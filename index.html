  • If the outcome of the roll is 1, the user looses all the points he gathered rolling the dice.
  • If the person fears that the outcome will be 1, he can click on hold button, and all the points he/she gathered will be added to the total points. And the second use plays.

The person who reaches 100 first will be the winner of the game.
